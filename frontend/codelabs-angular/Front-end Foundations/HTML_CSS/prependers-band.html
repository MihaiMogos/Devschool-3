The Prependers

Bio

The Prependers are a quirky four-piece from Orlando, FL. Critics have htrailed them as the greatest post-garage-rock band of the last decade.

They are currently writing a full length album that's a follow-up to their breakout 2011 album, Top Right Bottom Left, and their critically acclaimed EP, Opacity Zero.

  • Sarah Quan - Guitar
